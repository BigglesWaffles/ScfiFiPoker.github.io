Sci Fi Poker - Script Version 1

Sci Fi Poker Game.

Main theme for actors: Costume Drama (fancy futuristic outfits). Playing around on the banks or the river wey with a hire boat. Between 5 and 7 actors (possibly one or two tiny parts can be AI generated):

William and 3 or 4 card players (dressed up in sci-fi outfits – various unsavoury characters – male and female. Smaller parts: AI Bot. Security Guard, Croupier, and Doctor. (possibly AI if not enough volunteers)

1. Scene One - GARDEN

William Smith: collapses in his garden. 10 Seconds.

2. Scene Two GREEN SCREEN / BUSY HOSPITAL

William Smith: wakes up in a hospital “Where am I…”

Doctor: Enters ward. Nutty Professor type voice (Germanic perhaps). He speaks fast not giving William any time to interject or ask questions.

Doctor: “Hello William I have news for you. You have a very rare condition. The quantum entanglement of your brain’s neurons is not matching the structure of your heart. The two organs at a subatomic level are out of step. If we don’t correct this problem you will die, very soon, an hour, possibly two.”

William Smith: Shocked – wants to speak but can’t get the words out.

Doctor: The Doctor offers Wiliam a tablet. “This is very new, hush-hush, pre-clinical trial, a temporary fix. It will give you ONE extra hour of life. But there is cost, your mind or body might experience an effect not that dissimilar to time travel”.

William Smith: feels he has no alternative, so he swallows the offered tablet. He passes out again. FADE 1m: 00

3. Scene Three – ROWING BOAT NEAR FLYOVER FOOTPATH.

William wakes up in the future. In a rowing boat with an AI Bot (this Bot can be AI generated or it can be an actor – depending on how many people we have).

William Smith: Is lying in the boat. He slowly wakes up and sees the AI Bot. William sits up and looks at his phone. It says 30 minutes have elapsed. William shakes his head in disbelief. He only has 30 minutes left to live.

AI Bot: “I have analysed your condition and as a result you are in this boat”.

William Smith: “What’s… What’s going on?”

AI Bot: “Do not worry, a full cure is available at the “Network” on WEY Island.….”

AI Bot pauses, then continues “Well, provided you play their game.”

William Smith: “What Game?”

AI Bot: you will need to win a round of ‘Medical Poker’ if you want the cure..”.

William Smith: Acts dazed and confused while the Bot rows. FADE 30 seconds

4. Scene Four – “WEY ISLAND” UNDER THE FLYOVER FOOTPATH.

William Smith: Jumps out of the boat and ties it to a tree.

Security Guard: Blocks William from going further then says: “You’ll need 200 ZOTS to gain entry to the game”.

William Smith: He looks at his phone it now says 40mins. He has only 20 minutes of life left. William Smith takes a desperate gamble “ Will you buy the boat? It’s worth 200 ZOTS. “

Security Guard: looks at the boat but he is not impressed. But his eye catches sight of the Bots laser gun. “I’ll tell you what, if you give me the boat and the Bot’s gun I’ll give you 200 ZOTS.

William Smith: Nods in agreement. He walks to the “Network” building and leaves the Bot behind.

AI Bot: shouts: “Ask for QuantumSlimVac” FADE 1m.00

5. Scene five – INSIDE THE MEDICAL POKER GAME

Medical Poker: The teams bet money like real poker but whenever they win a hand they get a drug of their choice. Gangsters are here to make money from the desperately ill. There is a croupier at the door.

Croupier: “What medication do you require?”

William Smith: “QuantumSlimVac” William then goes and sits at a table with three other players. The other three each have a laser pistol.

Croupier: Walks over to the table and places the tablets on the table. “Good luck everyone” . She shuffles the cards and then deals them out.

William thinks he has a good hand. He bets 20 ZOTS. He looks at his mobile. 50 minutes have gone. Only 10 minutes left.

PLAYER1 (female drug gangster) : She looks at her hand. It’s a “Full house”. She bets 40 ZOTS. Other players fold but William bets another 20 ZOTS to see her hand. He loses and she takes the money and her drug from the table.

PLAYER1 (female drug gangster): “This is easy” – she carries on playing.

Croupier: “No hands on rayguns” – She waits until they move then she deals.

PLAYER2 (male playboy looking for danger): He has four aces. He looks pleased. “Oh boy” he states before betting 50 ZOTS.

William Smith: Looks at his cards and his phone (53 minutes gone). He hopes the player is bluffing . William bets 50 ZOTS. The others fold. PLAYER2 wins. Its now 55 mins gone.

Croupier: “All hands on the table pleas” (she looks at William who has his phone under the table looking at the time 57minutes gone).

William: Stumbles out “Sorry” and puts his phone back.

PLAYER3 (male evil looking dude): He has a “Straight flush” – he thinks he has won. He bets 100ZOTS….. The others fold but…

William: He looks at his cards. He knows it must be 59 minutes by now. He bets 100 ZOTs too.

PLAYER3: Sensing blood bets another 10 ZOTS to finish William off.

William: Bets the extra 10 ZOTS and calls.

PLAYER3: Places his straight flush on the table. He goes to grab the money and his medication.

William: Says “Wait” and he places his cards down – “It’s a Royal Flush”. HE WINS!

PLAYER1 2 and 3 accuse William of cheating.. they all go for their rayguns. Its now 59minutes and 55seconds.

William: madly grabs the drugs and bungs the lot into his mouth simultaneously the drug lords fires their laser pistols and shoots William – his phone alarm rings, it an hour.... 2.5 mins

6. Scene Six

William: wakes up in the garden just a bit dizzy – not too sure if anything has happened. He has something from the future in his hand – it’s the “Royal Flush”. 15 secs

THE END..
